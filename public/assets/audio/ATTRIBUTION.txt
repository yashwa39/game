AUDIO ASSETS - ATTRIBUTION REQUIRED

===========================================
SOUND EFFECTS
===========================================

When you add sound effects, please include attribution:

Recommended Sources:
- Zapsplat.com (Free with account, requires attribution)
- Freesound.org (CC0 or CC BY licensed)
- Pixabay.com (Free for commercial use)

Required SFX:
- button_click.mp3 (or .wav, .ogg)
- success.mp3
- fail.mp3

===========================================
BACKGROUND MUSIC
===========================================

When you add background music, please include attribution:

Recommended Sources:
- Bensound.com (Royalty Free, requires attribution)
- Incompetech.com (Kevin MacLeod, CC BY)
- Pixabay.com (Free for commercial use)

Required:
- background_loop.mp3 (or .wav, .ogg) - Calming instrumental loop

===========================================
LICENSE NOTES
===========================================

Always verify the license for each asset:
- CC0 = Public Domain (no attribution required, but appreciated)
- CC BY = Creative Commons Attribution (attribution required)
- Royalty Free = Usually requires attribution in credits

Add attribution to:
1. README.md (Credits section)
2. Footer of index.html (optional)
3. Game credits screen (if applicable)
